⚔ Build Your Raid Composition

📋 General TBC 25-Man Template
Tanks (2–3)
  • 2× Protection Warrior — MT and OT
  • 1× Protection Paladin — AoE/add threat
Healers (6–7)
  • 2× Holy Paladin — tank healing + blessings
  • 2× Restoration Druid — HoTs, Innervate
  • 2× Holy Priest — raid healing, PoH
  • 1× Restoration Shaman — Chain Heal, Bloodlust
DPS (15)
  • 2× Affliction Warlock — CoE, replenishment
  • 2× Mage — AI, AoE, Bloodlust group
  • 2× Shadow Priest — VT replenishment
  • 2× Hunter — Misdirection, Aspect of Wild
  • 2× Rogue — Combat burst
  • 2× Enhancement Shaman — Windfury, BL
  • 2× Balance Druid — Moonkin, Faerie Fire
  • 1× Elemental Shaman — Bloodlust coverage
💡 Must-Have Buffs
Bloodlust Coverage Bring 3+ Shamans spread across groups. Assign BL timing per boss.
Shadow Priests Mandatory from SSC onward. 2+ SPriests dramatically sustain healer mana on long fights.
Resist Sets Hydross needs Frost+Nature resist tanks. Mother Shahraz needs 365 SR for all 25 players.
Buff Checklist
  • Arcane Intellect · Gift of the Wild
  • Blessing of Kings / Might / Wisdom
  • Fortitude · Divine Spirit
  • Faerie Fire / Sunder Armor
  • Commanding / Devotion Aura
  • All 4 totem types per group

High King Maulgar & Council
PhysicalMagic5 Tanks
▾
5 Tanks Required Each council member needs a dedicated tank. Assign before pull.
Kill Order
  • 1. Krosh Firehand — hardest to tank (Warlock tank with Mage reflect)
  • 2. Olm the Summoner — kill his Fel Seekers instantly
  • 3. Blindeye the Seer — interrupt Heal and fear
  • 4. Kiggler the Crazed — interrupt Spell Flay nonstop
  • 5. High King Maulgar — tank swap when MT is polymorphed
Key Abilities
AbilitySourceResponse
Fireball BarrageKroshMage reflects back to sustain Warlock tank
Summon Fel SeekerOlmKill seekers instantly before they reach raid
Spell FlayKigglerInterrupt EVERY cast — assign 2 rogues/warriors
Greater PolymorphMaulgarOT immediately taunts — MT runs back when unsheeped
WhirlwindMaulgarAll melee run out during spin
⚔ Tank Tasks
  • Tank 1 (Prot War): MT High King Maulgar — back against wall, watch for polymorph
  • Tank 2 (Prot War): Tank Olm — face away from raid, call out Fel Seekers
  • Tank 3 (Prot Pal): Tank Kiggler — pull to far corner, maintain aggro through silences
  • Tank 4 (any): Tank Blindeye — interrupt Heal on CD, Fear Ward yourself
  • Warlock Tank: Stand near Mage, spam Hellfire/Siphon Life while Mage reflects fireballs
💚 Healer Tasks
  • HPal 1: Dedicated heals on Maulgar's tank — biggest damage intake
  • HPal 2: Heals on Krosh tank (Warlock) — he takes heavy damage
  • RDruid 1+2: Raid HoTs across all active tanks and melee group
  • HPriest: Dispel arcane debuffs, raid healing on cleave damage
  • RSham: Chain Heal on melee group, Bloodlust at 50% on Maulgar
⚡ DPS Tasks
  • Mage 1: Stand next to Warlock — Spell Reflect on CD (critical for Krosh tank)
  • Rogue 1+2: 100% kick uptime on Kiggler's Spell Flay — rotate kicks
  • All DPS: Focus fire kill order: Krosh → Olm → Blindeye → Kiggler → Maulgar
  • Hunters: Misdirect adds to assigned tanks, kill Fel Seekers immediately
  • SPriests: Maintain VT on Maulgar for mana return throughout fight
📍 Maulgar Council — Positioning Map
Positioning Key All 5 bosses are split to corners/walls so AoE from one doesn't hit tanks of another. The Warlock+Mage pair for Krosh must stay tightly together.

Hydross the Unstable
NatureFrostResist Sets Required
▾
Resist Gear Required Frost tank needs 365 Frost Resist cap. Nature tank needs 365 Nature Resist cap. Craft these before attempt.
Mechanics
  • Hydross walks between Frost (north) and Nature (south) — each crossing spawns 4 elemental adds
  • Phase transition happens when tank walks him across the center beam on the floor
  • Transition at 6 stacks of the phase debuff — no earlier, no later
  • Adds must die before DPS resumes on Hydross
  • Frost Form: Frost damage aura — whole raid needs some frost resist gear
  • Nature Form: Poison Nova — dispellers must cleanse immediately
⚔ Tank Tasks
  • Frost Tank (War, 365 FR): Take Hydross to north side — hold until 6 stacks then walk south across center
  • Nature Tank (War, 365 NR): Waiting south — taunt immediately as Hydross crosses center
  • Both tanks: Pre-position on correct side before pull
💚 Healer Tasks
  • 2 healers follow each tank — move north/south with tank during transitions
  • Cleanse Poison Nova from entire raid during Nature phase
  • Transition burst: All 4 adds deal AoE damage — pre-shield/HoT raid
⚡ DPS Tasks
  • STOP DPS on Hydross 3 seconds before transition — threat resets on phase change
  • Immediately switch to and burn 4 elemental adds on every transition
  • Resume DPS on Hydross only after all adds are dead
  • Frost resist gear recommended for all DPS
📍 Hydross — North/South Phase Positioning
