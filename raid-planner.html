⚔ Build Your Raid Composition

📋 Recommended Composition
Tanks (2–3)
  • 2× Protection Warrior — Main and off-tank
  • 1× Protection Paladin — AoE threat / add control
Healers (6–7)
  • 2× Holy Paladin — Tank healing + blessings
  • 2× Restoration Druid — HoT coverage, Innervate
  • 2× Holy Priest — Raid healing, Prayer of Healing
  • 1× Restoration Shaman — Chain Heal, Bloodlust
DPS (15–16)
  • 1–2× Affliction Warlock — Curse of Elements, Shadow Bolts
  • 1× Demonology Warlock — Bloodlust alternative
  • 2× Mage — Arcane Intellect, AoE
  • 2× Shadow Priest — Vampiric Touch, replenishment
  • 2× Hunter — Misdirection, Aspect of Wild
  • 2× Rogue — Combat swords for burst
  • 2× Enhancement Shaman — Windfury, Bloodlust
  • 1–2× Balance Druid — Moonkin aura, faerie fire
💡 Composition Notes
Bloodlust / Heroism Bring at least 3 Shamans to cover all 3 groups. Stagger Bloodlust: pull → phase → execute.
Curse Coverage Ensure a Warlock has Curse of Elements up for all magic-damage bosses. Ele/Resto shamans can provide Curse of Elements resist aura alternative.
Shadow Priests At least 2 Shadow Priests drastically improve mana sustain. They are mandatory for long fights like Archimonde and Illidan.
Must-Have Buffs Checklist
  • Arcane Intellect (Mage)
  • Gift of the Wild (Druid)
  • Blessing of Kings / Might / Wisdom (Paladin)
  • Fortitude / Divine Spirit (Priest)
  • Commanding / Devotion Aura
  • Faerie Fire / Sunder Armor (for physical DPS)
  • Totem coverage all 3 groups

Magtheridon's Lair

25-Player · Hellfire Citadel · 1 Boss
Tier 4 Head Token Magtheridon drops head tokens for Tier 4 helm. Clickers are the #1 cause of wipes — practice the click rotation in advance.
😈
Magtheridon
Fire Shadow 5 Clickers Required
▾
Clickers — 5 players must click their Cube of Imprisonment simultaneously every time Magtheridon casts Blaze. Missing a click = Channeling disruption = raid wipes from uncontrolled Blaze.
⚔ Phase 1 Tanks
  • 5 Channelers — one tank each
  • Face channelers away from raid
  • Be ready to move to Magtheridon in P2
💚 Healers
  • 1 healer per channeler tank
  • Raid heal during Quake (P2)
  • 1 healer assigned as backup clicker
⚡ DPS
  • Burn channelers fast in P1
  • Priority: kill all 5 before P2
  • 5 DPS assigned as cube clickers
Phase Breakdown
PhaseDurationKey Events
Phase 1~2 minKill 5 Hellfire Channelers — interrupt their casts, DPS fast
Phase 2Until 30%Magtheridon active — Blaze of Inferno every 60s requires cube click
Phase 330% → 0%Magtheridon frenzies + Quakes — burn hard, increased collapse damage
Cube Click Mechanics (Critical)
  • Assign 5 players to specific cubes — position them BEFORE the pull
  • Clickers must stand within 5 yards of their cube at all times
  • Raid Leader calls "CLICK" on voice comms or use a macro with /rw
  • All 5 click within 1 second of each other — this silences Magtheridon
  • Clickers maintain DPS between Blaze casts — do NOT stand idle
  • Assign a 6th backup clicker in case someone dies

Serpentshrine Cavern

25-Player · Coilfang Reservoir · Tier 5 · 6 Bosses
Tier 5 Raid SSC and TK must both be cleared to unlock Mount Hyjal. Lady Vashj requires collecting 4 Tainted Cores — have a clear hand-off plan.
💧
Hydross the Unstable
NatureFrost2 Tanks — Resist Sets
▾
Resist Gear Required — MT needs 365 Frost Resist (capped) in Frost form. OT needs 365 Nature Resist in Nature form. Both tanks must have these sets crafted before attempting.
Mechanics
  • Hydross switches between Frost (north) and Nature (south) phases when moved across the center
  • Each phase transition spawns 4 elemental adds — kill these FAST before DPS resumes on Hydross
  • Tank him exactly on the line — crossing too deep triggers adds too fast
  • Frost tank takes him north, Nature tank takes him south on transitions
  • Melee stack on Hydross only when in the correct phase for their resist set
  • Transition at 6 stacks of the phase debuff — do not let it stack past 6
🌊
The Lurker Below
PhysicalNature
▾
Fishing Encounter — The Lurker is fished up from the center grate. Use Aquatic Form or water walking to position. One of the more accessible SSC bosses.
Mechanics
  • Spout rotates 360° — everyone jumps into the water to avoid it. Watch the animation and pre-jump.
  • Whirl: Cleave to melee — melee run back out, ranged stay at range
  • Geyser: Targeted player is launched into the air then falls into water. Not deadly alone.
  • Submerge (at ~100 energy): Lurker submerges, adds spawn on the platforms — kill them before Lurker returns
  • Assign groups to each platform — do NOT let adds run rampant
  • 3 Coilfang Ambushers per platform — CC one, kill the other, then mop up
🌀
Leotheras the Blind
ShadowFireDemon Form
▾
Inner Demon — Each player who gets Inner Demon must kill their own Demon copy or they are mind-controlled for the rest of the fight. Every player must be able to kill their demon.
Mechanics
  • Leotheras alternates between Humanoid (physical) and Demon (shadow) forms every 45 sec
  • Humanoid Form: Only a Warrior tank can hold aggro. He Whirlwinds — all melee run OUT
  • Demon Form: Warrior loses aggro — a Warlock or Demo-specced player tanks him. Do not nuke in this phase
  • Inner Demon: 1–3 random players get a demon copy — they must kill it themselves. Others cannot help.
  • Chaos Blast: Frontal cone in Demon form — tank positions Leo facing away from raid
  • Assign Warlock tank and prep them with full health before demon phase

  • Kill Order: Sharkkis (hunter pet) → Tidalvess (shaman) → Caribdis (healer) → Karathress last
  • Karathress gains abilities from each dead advisor — kill in correct order to minimize danger

  • Tidal Wave: Massive AoE frost damage — raid stacks frost resist aura/totem
  • Watery Grave: 4 players are teleported into the water with Murloc adds
  • Assign 4 DPS "grave teams" — they must kill their murlocs before rejoining raid

Lady Vashj
NatureTainted CoresLegendary Boss
▾
Most Complex Boss in SSC — Phase 2 requires coordination, positioning, and flawless Core hand-offs. Assign and practice roles before pull.
⚔ Tanks
  • MT: Vashj (P1 & P3)
  • P2: 4 add tanks at generators
  • Assign tank per Elite add spawn
💚 Healers

  • P2: Heal each add tank group

  • P2: Kill adds, collect cores, kite striders

  • 4 Generators must be deactivated — each requires a Tainted Core
  • Tainted Cores drop from Tainted Elementals — assign DPS to each quadrant to kill them
  • Only 1 player can hold a Core at a time (debuffs you, slows movement speed)
  • Pre-assign a chain: Collector → Runner → Depositor for each generator
  • Cores must be THROWN using right-click on a player, not walked to the generator
  • Once all 4 generators are down, Vashj becomes attackable again — Phase 3 begins
  • Coilfang Striders: Must be kited and slowed — assign Hunters with Frost Trap rotation
  • Enchanted Thrall (MC): A Warlock or Hunter must enslave or trap them

Tempest Keep: The Eye

25-Player · Netherstorm · Tier 5 · 4 Bosses
Tier 5 Raid Kael'thas is one of the most iconic boss fights in TBC. Assign roles for each weapon phase — it is extremely complex.
🤖
Al'ar (Phoenix God)
FirePhysical
▾
Mechanics
  • Phase 1: Al'ar flies between 8 platforms. Tanks must follow and pick him up at each platform
  • Tanks must be in position BEFORE he lands — pre-position on the platform he will move to
  • Flame Buffet: Stacking fire dot — tanks need FR gear and swap when stacks get too high
  • Meteor: Targeted AoE — spread raid slightly to minimize splash
  • Phase 2 (75%): Al'ar lands in the center. Standard tank and spank with adds
  • Phoenix Rebirth: When Al'ar "dies" in P2, he turns to an egg — nuke the egg before it hatches
  • Dive Bomb in P2: Targeted player takes massive damage — ensure all DPS have health above 50%

Void Reaver
PhysicalArcane
▾
Loot Reaver — Void Reaver is the most accessible TK boss and often killed first for gear. His only dangerous mechanic is Arcane Orb.
Mechanics
  • Arcane Orb: Slow-moving orb targeting random raid members — run away from it. Never run toward the raid.
  • Pounding: AoE damage + knock-back every 15 seconds. Melee take more damage — healers spam during this
  • Knock Away: Tank gets knocked back — OT immediately picks up Reaver
  • Raid spreads loosely to not have Orbs chain-hit multiple targets
  • Fairly tank-and-spank — focus on Orb awareness and you will down him

Kael'thas Sunstrider
FireArcane5-Phase FightLegendary
▾
Hardest Pre-Hyjal Boss — 5 phases including advisor council, weapon phase, and full Kael'thas. Assign all roles for P3 (weapons) before pull. This fight takes weeks to learn.
Phase Breakdown
PhaseSummaryPriority Action
P1: Advisors4 advisors, each requires tank + interruptsKill all 4 before Kael lands at ~50% each
P2: Weapons9 legendary weapons spawn on groundPick up & equip assigned weapons, defeat empowered weapons
P3: Kael'thas ActiveAdvisors rez, Kael attacksDPS Kael to 50%, AoE adds down
P4: Gravity LapseEntire raid floats in the airHealers sustain floating players — Kael spams Pyroblast

Phase 2 Weapon Assignments (Critical)
  • Pre-assign every weapon to a specific player BEFORE the pull
  • Weapons: Infinity Blade (MT), Phaseshift Bulwark (tank), Warp Slicer (melee), Devastation (melee), Netherstrand Longbow (hunter), Nether Beam (healer), etc.
  • Each weapon MC's its holder initially — raiders must damage the weapon to break the MC
  • Once weapons are picked up, they animate and attack — kill them in order
  • Do NOT pick up a weapon if you are not assigned — chaos will result
  • Assign backup players for each weapon in case the primary dies
Gravity Lapse (Phase 4)
  • All players float in the air — movement is reduced, positioning is chaotic
  • Kael spams Pyroblast — interrupt with assigned interrupters cycling casts
  • Healers must keep all 25 players alive — this is the most healing-intensive moment
  • Assign 3 Pyroblast interrupters rotating every cast
  • Gravity Lapse ends after ~30 sec — be ready to resume DPS immediately

25-Player · Caverns of Time · Tier 6 · 5 Bosses
Tier 6 Raid Mount Hyjal is primarily a wave-defense encounter before each boss. AoE DPS is highly valued. Trash packs are very dangerous — do not sleep on them.

  • Frost Nova: Roots nearby players — healers must be mobile to get out

  • Standard positioning: Tank boss, ranged spread, be ready to move for Death & Decay
  • Assign 2 DPS to immediately attack any Icebolted player to free them

  • Vampiric Aura: Anetheron heals from melee hits — use ranged heavy DPS comp or accept the healing
  • Infernals that land continue to burn their area — do not stack in areas with multiple Infernals

Kaz'rogal
ShadowPhysical
▾
Mana Check — Kaz'rogal periodically drains all mana to 0. Players with 0 mana after the drain EXPLODE dealing raid-wide damage. Healers and mana classes must use their mana before drain hits.
Mechanics
  • Mark of Kaz'rogal: Drains mana over time — if mana reaches 0, player explodes for massive AoE damage
  • When marked, players must immediately begin dumping all mana (spam everything)
  • Healers should pause healing for a second after getting marked to drain their own mana faster
  • Whirlwind: Tank moves him out of melee before Whirlwind begins
  • Cleave: Standard cleave — OT and melee stand behind
  • Assign a Warlock to Life Tap on cooldown — no mana = no explosion risk

Azgalor
ShadowFireDoom
▾
Mechanics
  • Doom: Places a 20-second countdown on a player. When it expires, they die and spawn a powerful Doomguard
  • The Doomguard must be immediately enslaved by a Warlock or will wipe the raid
  • Pre-assign 2 Warlocks to Enslave rotation on Doomguards
  • Players with Doom should not release — the Doomguard spawn is needed
  • Rain of Fire: AoE patch on random locations — move out constantly
  • Silence: Interupts all healing and casting — healers pre-cast and move during silence
  • Cleave: Tank faces away from raid

Black Temple

25-Player · Shadowmoon Valley · Tier 6 · 9 Bosses
Tier 6 Raid Black Temple is the pinnacle of TBC raiding. Illidan Stormrage is the final boss of the expansion. Many fights require specialized compositions and assignments.
🪨
High Warlord Naj'entus
NaturePhysical
▾
Mechanics
  • Tidal Shield: Naj'entus becomes immune with a shield bubble periodically. Must be broken with a Spine of Najentus
  • Impaling Spine: Stabs a random player with a spine — that player cannot move and takes DoT damage
  • The Spine must be removed from the player and thrown at Naj'entus to break the shield
  • Assign 1 player per quadrant to collect Spines from their area and throw them at the shield
  • Needle Spine: Spine thrown at random player — targeted player must move away from the raid
  • Shield break = massive AoE damage on raid — healers pre-cast heals for this moment

Supremus
FirePhysical
▾
Mechanics
  • Phase 1 (Tank & Spank): Standard tanking — melee behind, ranged spread
  • Volcano: Fire patches randomly appear — entire raid must move constantly to avoid them
  • Phase 2 (Chase Phase): Supremus loses aggro and chases random players — everyone must kite him
  • During chase phase: DO NOT let him catch you, kite in circles around the room
  • Tanks: Stay near Supremus to pick him up when Phase 1 resumes
  • Hateful Strike: In P1 — hits 2nd on threat list hard. OT must be 2nd in threat at all times

Shade of Akama
ShadowPhysical
▾
Mechanics
  • The Shade is initially immune — Akama's Soul Fragment (friendly NPC) must drain his essence
  • Ashtongue Broken (friendly adds) must escort Akama's fragment safely to the Shade

Teron Gorefiend
ShadowGhost Mechanic
▾
Ghost Players — Construct of Shadows kills a random player and turns them into a ghost. The ghost player must kill 4 Shadow Constructs within 30 seconds or they explode, killing the raid. Practice ghost control before the pull.
Ghost Phase (Critical)
  • When you become a ghost: Immediately target a Shadow Construct and spam your ghost abilities
  • You have 4 ghost abilities with short cooldowns — use them ALL to kill 4 constructs fast
  • Ghost players must position constructs away from the raid (they slow nearby players)
  • Living players: Ignore ghosts and keep DPS on Gorefiend
  • Shadow of Death: The debuff that creates ghosts. Note the name so you can warn the affected player
  • Incinerate: Massive single-target — designate a tank to absorb it
  • Practice ghost mechanics on test dummies or in a rehearsal before the real attempt
